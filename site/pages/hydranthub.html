HydrantHub

Hydrant asset management for small municipalities and property managers.

View Demo

Who it's for

  • Small municipalities
  • Property managers and facility owners
  • Industrial parks and campuses

Core features

  • Inventory management and NFPA classification
  • Map & GIS (GeoJSON export, Leaflet)
  • Testing workflows with NFPA 291 auto-calculations
  • Maintenance & inspections with history
  • Professional reporting (PDF)
  • Admin basics and audit trail

Technology

  • Secure cloud app with role-based access
  • API-compatible and export-friendly
