Features

Everything you need to manage hydrants, stay compliant, and deliver professional reports.

Inventory & Classification

  • Full hydrant profiles with NFPA class and status
  • Search, filters, and CSV export

Mapping & GIS

  • Interactive maps with clusters and detail panels
  • GeoJSON export and Leaflet integration

NFPA 291 Testing

  • Static/residual pressure and flow calculations
  • Auto-calculated GPM and color coding

Maintenance & Inspections

  • Inspection logging and condition tracking
  • Decommissioning and audit history

Reporting

  • Professional PDF test reports
  • Annual reporting packages

Security & Admin

  • Role-based access (admin, supervisor, operator)
  • Audit trail and session management
